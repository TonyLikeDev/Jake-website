# Golden Method Website – Final Wireframe & Developer Notes

## Hero Section
- **Headline:** Students Already Know More Than They Think  
- **Subheadline:** We help them express it clearly and confidently in English.  
- **Support:** Most students don’t struggle with understanding — they struggle with expression.  
- **CTA:** View Programs / Request a Trial Lesson  
- **Trust:** No commitment. Clear level assessment in first lesson.

## How Lessons Work
1. Calibrate the Right Level  
2. Build Confidence in Expression (focus on full sentences, reinforce effort, refine accuracy)  
3. Expand Beyond the Lesson  

**Key Idea:** Focus on expression, not just correctness.

## Programs (Learning Format)
- Group Classes – Most students continue here after assessment  
- 1–1 Consultant – Focused support  
- 1–1 with Jake – Premium, limited  
- IELTS Specialist – Score-focused preparation  

**Note:** Each leads to its own page (packages inside).

## Packages (Program Pages Only)
- 20 Lessons – Starter  
- 30 Lessons – Most Popular  
- 60 Lessons – Best Value  

**Note:** Do NOT show on homepage. Only inside program pages.

## What Makes This Different
- Students know more than they can express  
- Focus on clear expression first  
- Full sentence speaking  
- Confidence through communication  

## About Section
- Use “we” (team positioning)  
- 20+ years combined experience  
- Includes IELTS/TOEFL specialists  
- Focus: expression-first system, not memorization  

## Trial System
- Trial = assessment + placement  
- No instant booking  
- Manual scheduling after form submission  
- User submits availability via form  

## Form Fields
- Student info  
- Level  
- Goals  
- Availability  
- Speaking ability  
- Experience  
- Contact  
- Optional: “When do you want to start?”  

## User Flow
Land → Understand → Choose program → Go to program page → View packages → Submit trial request → Manual scheduling  

## Dev Rules
- No pricing on homepage  
- Packages only in program pages  
- Trial is request-based  
- Keep UI clean and premium
